Exotic Rainforest Animals

Here are some lesser-known rainforest animals.

  • Okapi
  • Glass Frog
  • Sun Bear

Okapi: This animal is a mammal that has stripes like a zebra but also a long tongue like a giraffe.

Glass Frog: This animal lives in rainforest trees and are pretty rare. Some species have transparent skin.

Sun Bear: This animal is the one of the smallest bear species. They are both nocturnal and omnivores.
